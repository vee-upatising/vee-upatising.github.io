About Me

  I am a very self-driven person and I'm motivated to refine my skills and learn constantly. I have a passion for Data Science and I find that I am very results-oriented. I like to make sure that my projects have visually interpretable results that anyone can understand.

 

  I graduated from the University of Massachusetts with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Engineering. I spend most of my free time coding personal projects or skateboarding. I find that each of these activities continuously pushes to me to work hard and become a better version of myself.

 

  I have been skateboarding since I was 11 years old. I spent a lot of time running the UMass Skateboarding Club during college. These are some photos showcasing the fun I had during that time period as well as a video I filmed during my senior year showcasing the skate community.
